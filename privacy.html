Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 January 2026

Wu Technologies ("we", "us", "our") is a UK-based technology company. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and your rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. We are registered with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

Who We Are and How to Contact Us

Wu Technologies is the data controller for personal data processed via this website and our company-operated services. We are registered with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO): ZC073339. To exercise your rights or ask questions about this policy, please contact us via our contact page.

What Personal Data We Collect

  • Website analytics: When you visit our site, we use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic and improve the site. This may collect pseudonymous identifiers, device information, and interaction events.
  • Contact enquiries: If you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the details you provide so we can respond.
  • Product-specific data: Certain products and services may collect additional data necessary to operate and secure the service. Product-specific privacy policies are linked from each product page.

Lawful Bases for Processing

  • Legitimate interests: Site analytics, security and performance monitoring.
  • Contract: To respond to enquiries you submit and to provide you with requested services.
  • Consent: Where required (for certain cookies/technologies or optional communications), we will request your consent and you may withdraw it at any time.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements.

How We Use Your Data

  • Operate, secure, and improve our website and services.
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Analyse aggregate and pseudonymous usage to improve performance and usability.
  • Maintain records necessary for compliance, security and audit.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use limited analytics cookies and browser APIs to understand site usage and improve performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Where required by UK law, we seek consent for non‑essential cookies/technologies.

Sharing and International Transfers

We may share personal data with trusted service providers acting on our behalf:

  • Google Analytics and reCAPTCHA: Site analytics and spam prevention. See Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms.
  • Formspree: Contact form processing. See Formspree’s Privacy Policy.

Some providers are located outside the UK. When we transfer personal data internationally, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses with the UK Addendum or other approved mechanisms.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Enquiry records are kept for a reasonable period to support follow‑up and quality assurance.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and obtain a copy.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erase your data in certain circumstances.
  • Restrict or object to processing, including processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Data portability where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the contact page. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO: ico.org.uk.

Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Product‑Specific Policies

Some products and services have dedicated privacy notices to address product‑specific processing. Please refer to each product’s page for details.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our operations. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please reach out via our contact page.
