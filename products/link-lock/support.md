## Managing Your Subscription

### How to Cancel Your Subscription

Your subscription is managed through your device's app store. Follow the instructions below based on your platform:

#### iOS (iPhone/iPad)

1. Open the **Settings** app on your device
2. Tap your **name** at the top of the screen
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find and tap **Link Lock** or **Mainframe Access**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**
6. Confirm the cancellation

Alternatively:
1. Open the **App Store** app
2. Tap your **profile icon** in the top right
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find and tap **Link Lock** or **Mainframe Access**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**
6. Confirm the cancellation

#### Android

1. Open the **Google Play Store** app
2. Tap the **menu icon** (three horizontal lines) in the top left
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find and tap **Link Lock** or **Mainframe Access**
5. Tap **Cancel subscription**
6. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm

Alternatively:
1. Go to **subscriptions.google.com**
2. Find **Link-Lock** or **Mainframe Access**
3. Click **Manage**
4. Click **Cancel subscription**

### When Does Cancellation Take Effect?

When you cancel your subscription:
- You will continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period
- You will not be charged for the next billing period
- Your subscription will automatically expire at the end of the current period

### How to Modify Your Subscription

To change your subscription plan (e.g., from monthly to annual):
1. Follow the steps above to access your subscriptions
2. Select **Link-Lock** or **Mainframe Access**
3. Choose a different subscription option
4. Confirm the change

### Refund Policy

Refund requests are handled by Apple or Google, depending on your platform:

#### iOS Refunds
1. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com/
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find your Link-Lock purchase
4. Select **Report a Problem**
5. Choose the reason for your refund request
6. Submit your request

Apple will review your request and respond directly.

#### Android Refunds
1. Go to https://play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory
2. Find your Link-Lock purchase
3. Click **Request a refund** or **Report a problem**
4. Follow the on-screen instructions

Google will review your request and respond directly.

**Note**: We do not control the refund process. All refund decisions are made by Apple or Google according to their policies.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

1. Install Link-Lock on your new device
2. Open the app
3. Go to **Settings** or **Account**
4. Tap **Restore Purchases**
5. Sign in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase

Your subscription will be restored automatically.

### Why isn't my subscription working?

If you've subscribed but don't have access to premium features:
1. Ensure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID or Google account
2. Try the **Restore Purchases** option in the app
3. Check your internet connection
4. Restart the app
5. If the issue persists, contact support@wu-tec.co.uk

### How do I change my email address?

To update your account email:
1. Open Link-Lock
2. Go to **Settings** or **Account**
3. Tap **Email** or **Account Information**
4. Enter your new email address
5. Verify the change through the confirmation email

Or contact support@wu-tec.co.uk for assistance.

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Open Link-Lock
2. Go to **Settings** or **Account**
3. Scroll to **Delete Account**
4. Follow the on-screen instructions

**Important**: Deleting your account will:
- Remove all your game progress and data
- Cancel your subscription (you must still cancel through your app store to avoid future charges)
- This action cannot be undone

Alternatively, email support@wu-tec.co.uk with your account details and request account deletion. We will process your request within 30 days.

### Multiplayer Issues

If you're experiencing issues with multiplayer features:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure you have an active **Mainframe Access** subscription
3. Restart the app
4. Check if there are any app updates available
5. Contact support@wu-tec.co.uk if issues persist

**Note**: Encrypted Tunnel features require a stable internet connection and an active Mainframe Access subscription.

### Technical Issues

For crashes, bugs, or other technical problems:
1. Ensure you're using the latest version of Link-Lock
2. Restart your device
3. Check for iOS/Android system updates
4. Clear the app cache (Android only)
5. Reinstall the app (your progress will be saved if you're signed in)

If problems continue, contact support@wu-tec.co.uk with:
- Your device model and OS version
- App version
- Description of the issue
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
