# SiteSync: UK-Compliant Daily Logs & AI Automation
## Automate site diaries and HMRC compliance inside HubSpot. Built for UK contractors who need real-time field data without the manual entry.

[!IMPORTANT]

Certification Status: This app is designed for the HubSpot 2025.2 Platform and utilizes Breeze AI for automated data parsing.

## User Guide
### Overview
SiteSync bridges the gap between the construction site and your CRM. It enables field teams to capture daily site activities, labor hours, and weather conditions directly within HubSpot, ensuring full compliance with UK Construction Industry Scheme (CIS) regulations.

### Key Features
 'Daily Log' CRM Card: Streamlined, mobile-responsive interface for site managers.

 Breeze AI Processing: Automatically parses site notes to extract labor hours and entities.

 CIS Compliance Flagging: Real-time validation of subcontractor status to reduce HMRC audit risks.

 Media Integration: One-click photo attachments with integrated GDPR consent tracking.

### How to Use
Navigate to any Deal (Project) or Company record in HubSpot.

Locate the SiteSync Daily Log card in the sidebar or dedicated tab.
